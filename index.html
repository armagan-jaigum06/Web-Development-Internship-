About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K. Rowling. The story follows the young wizard, Harry, and his friends Hermione and Ron as they navigate life at Hogwarts and battle the dark wizard Voldemort. This iconic series has inspired generations with its themes of friendship, bravery, and the triumph of good over evil.
